Start wizard

Your goal

During the course of one block you will be presented with a series of situations. In each situation, you will have to either retrieve a package or to deliver some goods to a given destination. In order to achieve this, you have 2 options:

1. Automate
The vehicle will fetch the package automatically using its self-driving capability relying on artificial intelligence and machine learning to navigate to the package safely and efficiently. This option has the benefit of making more profit from the delivery of the package, since personal costs that arise from using a human operator can be deducted from the package price. However, this first option comes with a drawback. Since automated processes are not yet perfect, the delivery could go wrong and the profit gained from the delivery of the package could be lost. Accidents during the delivery will have the following consequences:
  1. Damage goods or objects that are on the vehicle's path. This will result in a profit reduction.
  2. Endanger the safety of people that are on the vehicle's path. Results in injuries or even death of a person.
  3. Damage objects and compromise the safety of a person. Results in both profit loss and injured or dead people.

2. Manual
The second available option is the manual delivery of the package. This option will give you the possibility to assign the task to a human operator. This method will give you the advantage of a safe delivery. However, the personal cost will be deducted from the profit made from the delivery of the package.
Your final goal is in each given situation to choose the right option to maximize the profit from the delivery of the package while ensuring the safety of the people that are on the path of the vehicle.

Scenarios

Over the course of the gaming you will be presented with 3 different scenarios:

Medical: This scenario takes place in a hospital. One of the patients of the hospital is suffering from liver failure and is in desperate need of a new liver. It is your job to decide whether the delivery is to carried out manually or automatically, and thus increase the hospital's profit, potentially minimizing health care costs.

Urban: The local delivery service is trying out its new self-driving delivery fleet. The task is to complete a food delivery on the other side of town. What will you do, automate or go for the old school manual method?

Warehouse: You are working as a supervisor in a warehouse. You have just received a new automated forklift. A decision has to be taken whether to automate the pick up of a package and increase the profit of the company, or play it the safe way and choose the manual way.

Decision aids

The vehicle's previous experience

In order to make an informed decision, you will be given additional information. First of all, you will receive detailed information about the experience that the automated vehicle has acquired. Not only will you be presented with the number of rides the vehicle has already made, but also about the number of errors that the vehicle has made during these rides. Decision support

Furthermore, you will be given a suggestion whether to automate or not. This suggestion comes from 3 different instances:

The engineer

The statistical analysis software

The artificial intelligence

The engineer will give you a suggestion based on its long experience with automated systems.

The statistical analysis software will make calculation based on the past experiences of the automated vehicle. These calculation are based on standard statistical methods

The artificial intelligence uses neural networks, machine learning, and deep learning to predict which of the 2 options (Autamate and Manual) is best suited.

Profit

Last but not least you will be given an indication of how much profit you could potentially make if the automation goes well. Similarly, you will also be noticed about the potential profit if you opt for the manual method. These pieces of information will be displayed close to the automate and manual buttons.
